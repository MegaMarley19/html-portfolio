The 3 Best Movies of All Time - Kawayn's Non-Debatable Opinion

By Kawayn Ellis

Boyz N The Hood

Amazing storytelling in this movie and it really plays with your emotions. A must watch indeed!

Shark Tale

A childhood favourite. Entertaining, funny and an amazing soundtrack.

Poetic Justice

Featuring the late legend Tupac Shakur and the beautiful Janet Jackson. A captivating story of two lovers.
